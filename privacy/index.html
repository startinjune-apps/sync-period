Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

The most important thing to know: Your personal health data stays completely on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Introduction

Welcome to Sync Period, a menstrual cycle tracking app created by Start in June LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Personal Health Data (Stored On Your Device Only)

All personal health data is stored locally on your device. We do not process or store health data on our servers.

Analytics Data

We collect limited, anonymized analytics to understand how the app is used:

  • Screen opens: Which screens and sheets you open (e.g. calendar, stats, log sheet)
  • Basic device info: OS version and app version for compatibility
  • Anonymous identifier: A randomly generated ID is created on first launch and stored on your device. It is never linked to your identity and is used only to count unique users and understand usage patterns over time.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Any health data, cycle details, or log content
  • Attribution or advertising data

Crash Reports

If the app crashes, a report is automatically sent to Sentry (our crash reporting service) on the next app launch. Crash reports contain a stack trace and basic device context (OS version, app version). They do not contain any health data or personal information.

How We Use Your Information

Analytics Data

Analytics data are used to understand which features are used so we can improve the app. Processed by Amplitude, an industry-standard analytics platform.

How We Store and Protect Your Information

On Your Device

All personal health data is stored locally using secure on-device storage.

Analytics (Amplitude)

Anonymized screen-view events are processed by Amplitude. Amplitude is GDPR-compliant and follows strict privacy standards. No personal identifiers or health content are included in these events.

Crash Reports (Sentry)

Crash reports are processed by Sentry, an industry-standard error monitoring platform. Reports are sent only when a crash occurs and contain no health data or personal information.

Data Sharing

We never share your personal health data. It never leaves your device.

We do not:

  • Use advertising networks
  • Sell or share data with data brokers
  • Share data for marketing purposes
  • Track users across apps

Data Retention and Deletion

Personal health data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. Deleting the app removes all personal data from your device.

Analytics data is retained by Amplitude according to their data retention policies. To request deletion of your analytics data, contact us at startinjune.apps@gmail.com.

Your Rights

  • Access and control: You have complete control over your personal data on your device
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all personal health data
  • Analytics deletion: Email startinjune.apps@gmail.com to request deletion of analytics data
  • GDPR (EU users): All personal health data is stored locally on your device. We do not process or store health data on our servers. For questions about your rights under GDPR, contact us at startinjune.apps@gmail.com.
  • CCPA (California users): We do not sell your personal information

Third-Party Services

Amplitude Analytics

  • Purpose: App usage analytics and improvement
  • Data shared: Anonymized screen-view events and anonymous identifier only
  • Privacy: GDPR-compliant, no personal identifiers, IP address disabled
  • More info: Amplitude Privacy Policy

Sentry Crash Reporting

  • Purpose: Crash detection and app stability improvement
  • Data shared: Stack traces and basic device context (OS version, app version) on crash only
  • Privacy: No health data or personal identifiers included
  • More info: Sentry Privacy Policy

Age Requirement

Sync Period is intended for users aged 13 and older. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old.

International Users

Sync Period is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the collection and processing of anonymized analytics data as described in this policy. Your personal health data never leaves your device regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
